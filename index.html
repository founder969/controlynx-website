The Problem

Manual project controls
is costing you control.

Every construction project runs on the same broken reporting loop. Your best engineers spend their time on admin instead of analysis.

01

Reports take hours to produce

Planning engineers spend Monday mornings chasing data from site teams instead of reviewing the schedule. A report that should take 30 minutes takes half a day.

"I'll send the DPR by 3pm — still waiting on data from site."

02

Delays surface too late

By the time a delay is visible in a monthly report, the project has already lost two weeks. Early warning signals are buried in spreadsheets nobody has time to read.

"We only found out about the concrete delay when the client asked."

03

Data lives in silos

Progress data, manpower logs, procurement status, and engineering approvals all sit in separate Excel files, WhatsApp threads, and email chains. Nobody has the full picture.

"The MEP progress figures in the weekly report don't match what the subcontractor submitted."

04

Management lacks real-time visibility

Directors and PMOs receive weekly snapshots at best. They make decisions on data that is already five days old, and can't act on project health issues until it's too late to recover.

"By the time the monthly report reaches me, the problem is already a contract issue."

The Solution

From site data to executive report.
In minutes, not days.

Controlynx.ai collects, structures, and transforms your project data into professional reports and dashboards — automatically.

4

FAQ

Common questions from
project controls professionals.

Does Controlynx.ai replace project controls teams?
No. Controlynx.ai is a tool for project controls professionals, not a replacement for them. The platform handles the mechanical, time-consuming parts of the job — data collection, formatting, and report generation — so that engineers can focus on analysis, programme control, and risk management. Human review is built into every report before submission.
Can it work with our existing Excel-based workflows?
Yes. Controlynx.ai is designed to work alongside your current processes, not replace them. You can import schedule data from Excel, P6 exports, or MS Project files. Outputs are available as Excel, PDF, Word, and PowerPoint — so your team can work with them in existing tools without friction.
Can it generate PowerPoint presentations for client meetings?
Yes. The PowerPoint Report Generator produces a client-ready slide deck from your project data — including S-curve, progress charts, risk highlights, photo grid, and key metrics. You review and adjust before sharing. No more Sunday evening formatting.
Does it support Primavera P6 schedule data?
Controlynx.ai is designed to accept Primavera P6-style planning data including XER exports, activity lists, baseline schedules, and WBS hierarchies. Direct API integration with P6 is on the product roadmap. For current onboarding, structured imports are supported.
Does it generate daily, weekly, and monthly reports?
Yes. The core reporting modules cover Daily Progress Reports, Weekly Progress Reports, and Monthly Executive Reports. Additional outputs include delay analysis reports, resource reports, engineering logs, procurement logs, cost reports, and PowerPoint presentations — all generated from the same underlying project data.
How is project data kept secure?
Your project data belongs to you. It is never used to train external AI models, shared with third parties, or used outside the scope of your account. All data is handled with enterprise-grade security standards. For enterprise clients, dedicated data residency and enhanced security arrangements are available.
Is it suitable for GCC-based contractors and consultants?
Yes. Controlynx.ai was built by a construction professional with 22 years of experience in GCC construction markets. Report formats, contract terminology, and workflow assumptions reflect GCC construction industry standards. The platform is designed for global deployment and currently serving clients across the GCC region.
