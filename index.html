The problem

Monday morning.
Every week. The same chaos.

Every construction project in the world runs on the same broken workflow. Every planning engineer knows exactly what we mean.

01

Data collection takes all morning

Your planning engineer spends Monday morning on WhatsApp and phone calls — chasing site teams and subcontractors for progress data that should have arrived automatically.

"I'll submit the report by 3pm — just waiting on data from site."

02

Reports go out with missing data

The data collection is never fully complete by the time the report is due. Every report contains estimates, gaps, and figures that are already outdated the moment they are submitted.

"I'll update the numbers when I get confirmation from the subcontractor."

03

40% of the week spent on formatting

Planning engineers are hired to analyse schedules, identify risks, and protect the programme. Instead, they spend nearly half their week copy-pasting data into Word and Excel templates.

"I didn't get time to review the programme this week — the monthly report took three days."

How it works

From site data to
client-ready report in minutes

Three steps. No manual formatting. No chasing data. No missed submissions.

1

Step 1

Site teams fill a 5-minute form

A simple mobile-friendly form — activities completed, manpower deployed, issues, photos. Takes less than 5 minutes. Works on any phone, no app required.

2

Step 2

AI generates the complete report

Controlynx reads the data, applies your project template, and generates a complete, professional report — daily, weekly, or monthly — with narrative, charts, and summaries.

3

Step 3

Review, approve, and send

Your engineer reviews the draft in minutes, makes any adjustments, and exports as PDF or Word. Client-ready. Every time. Without a Sunday evening spent on PowerPoint.

What's included

Every report your project needs.
Automated.

Four reporting modules — each one eliminating hours of manual work every week.

Daily

Daily Progress Report

Auto-generated from site team inputs. Complete, formatted, and client-ready every morning before your engineer finishes their coffee.

  • Activities completed and in progress
  • Manpower deployed by trade
  • Issues, delays, and weather log
  • Photo attachments auto-embedded
  • PDF and Word export
Weekly

Weekly Progress Report

Aggregated from 7 days of DPR data. S-curve, planned vs actual, lookahead, issues summary — all formatted and ready.

  • Planned vs actual S-curve
  • Progress by area and discipline
  • Issues and risks summary
  • 2-week lookahead schedule
  • Formatted per your client template
Monthly

Monthly Progress Report

The full executive report — narrative, KPIs, schedule variance, cost update, and forecast — generated in minutes instead of three days.

  • Executive narrative auto-written
  • Schedule and cost KPIs
  • Variance analysis with reasons
  • Next month forecast section
  • Risk and issues register
Presentation

Weekly Progress Presentation

The Monday morning client meeting deck — built automatically. Charts, metrics, photos, and key messages. No more Sunday evening PowerPoint sessions.

  • Auto-built slide deck
  • S-curve and progress charts
  • Key metrics and highlights
  • Photo grid from site
  • Export as PDF or PowerPoint
